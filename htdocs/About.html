Algorithm

X-ray reflectivity is calculated using the Parratt scheme.
L. C. Parratt, Surface Studies of Solid by Total Reflection of X-Rays, Phys. Rev., 93(2), 359 (1954).
The x-ray absorption coefficients are from,
B. L. Henke, E. M. Gullikson, and J. C. Davis, Atomic Data and Nuclear Data Tables 54(2) (1993).
